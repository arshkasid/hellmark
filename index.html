Who we are & What we do?

Welcome to the IIM L Store (a.k.a HellMark) - the official merchandise & souvenirs store of IIM Lucknow! We take pride in crafting your 'Life at L' into cherished memories by offering an exquisite range of campus merchandise and souvenirs. Whether you're a current student, alum, professor, staff, or an aspiring member, we believe everyone deserves to "wear the pride and join the legacy at L"
